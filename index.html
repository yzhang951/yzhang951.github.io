Welcome to Zhang Research group

Our group investigates the mechanical behavior of advanced engineering materials and energy materials at the nano to macro-scale. We focus on materials modeling and simulations.

The current research projects include the mechanics of ultra-strength materials; 3D-printed metal alloys; electrode degradation in lithium-ion batteries; strengthening mechanisms in high-entropy alloys; machine-learning assisted constitutive modeling.
